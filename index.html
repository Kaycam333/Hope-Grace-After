About the Organizer

The organizer has lived a life dedicated to kindness, honesty, and helping others. Yet, as someone who identifies as LGBTQ, they know that their homosexual lifestyle means they will be left behind after the Rapture. Instead of despair, they have chosen devotion: dedicating themselves to helping others who remain find God, embrace goodness, and seek grace in a world forever changed. With empathy born of their own struggle, they are committed to guiding others toward hope, faith, and a renewed path.

What We Offer

Spiritual & Emotional Support

Faith-based guidance sessions, prayer circles, and one-on-one mentorship to help navigate life after the rapture.

Safety & Resource Coordination

CB radio channel "7" will be utilized to coordinate meeting places and important messages and will broadcast scripture and motivational commentary to help those unable to travel find grace and comfort.

Community Building

Organizing support groups and community gatherings centered on hope, dignity, and grace.

Support the Mission

Your donations help provide support, hope and outreach for those left behind after the Rapture of September 23, 2025. Every contribution makes a difference. Your donation directly fuels a mission of compassion, hope, and faith for those left behind. In anycase where the rapture does not occur on September 23 or 24 of 2025, no refunds will be available and the organizer will destribute such funds as they see fit. In the wake of unimaginable loss and confusion, many will be searching for meaning, stability, and guidance. By contributing to this cause, you are helping provide counseling, spiritual outreach, and a safe space for people struggling to reconcile their past with their future. Every gift becomes an act of mercy — extending a hand to the hurting, bringing light into dark places, and offering the tools and encouragement needed to find God, rebuild lives, and rediscover purpose. No refunds. .
